Democratize [\di-ˈmä-krə-ˌtīz\] To make (something) available to all people

Democracy [\dɪˈmɑkɹəsi\] Form of government in which all eligible citizens are meant to participate equally

Agora Voting is an open source voting software that allows any organization to carry out secure and transparent electoral processes in a flexible way and at an affordable cost

software

Used in elections with 150.000+ voters.

security

We use algorithms and implement solutions published by leading voting process experts and academics from the most renowned institutions around the world

transparency

Our code-base can be reviewed by anyone and understand how the software works. Other solutions based on proprietary software apply the obsolete "security by obscurity" principle and act like black boxes.

flexibility

It is possible to configure almost any characteristic of the voting process. Our modular architecture makes it possible to extend the platform with plug-ins that add functionality such as authentication methods or reporting capabilities.

affordability

Our software is published as free software at no cost, so you don't have to pay any license fee, that usually represents a relevant part of the election costs.
